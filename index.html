Lusignan Mothers — The Daughters of the House

The Lusignan genetic legacy spread primarily through its daughters, who married into noble families across Europe. Each marriage carried Lusignan blood into new territories — England, Scotland, Savoy, Cyprus, the Holy Land, and eventually New France and New England. The 21 documented Lusignan mothers below represent the principal transmission points from the medieval dynasty to living descendants.
